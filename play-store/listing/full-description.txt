StrataSpent is the easiest way to track shared expenses with your family or group.

Whether you're managing the household budget, splitting a dinner bill, or
keeping tabs on monthly subscriptions, StrataSpent keeps everyone on the
same page in real time.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Create groups — invite household members by email; everyone sees the
  same expenses and balances live.
• Log expenditures by category — Food, Housing, Transport, Entertainment,
  and more.
• Scan receipts with AI — point your camera at any receipt and the app
  uses Google Gemini to pull out the amount, date, and category.
• Speak it instead — tap the microphone and say "Cincuenta pesos en
  comida ayer"; the app understands and fills the form.
• Split bills with a tap — one-time bar tab or group dinner? Spin up a
  temporary split, scan the receipt, assign items to people, see who
  owes what.
• Per-user financial flow — track your own income and personal spending
  alongside the group view.
• Reminders that ping you — never forget rent, utilities, or that
  recurring subscription. Tap "Pay" when it's due and the expense is
  logged automatically.
• Works offline — scan or speak when there's no signal; everything syncs
  the moment you're back online.
• Pick your currency — display formatting in USD, EUR, MXN, COP, BRL,
  ARS, and more.
• Live sync with the web app — the same shared database powers both
  Android and web (https://strataspent-124617071143.us-east1.run.app).

PRIVACY

StrataSpent uses Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Your data
stays under your Firebase project. Receipt images sent to Google Gemini
for OCR are processed and not stored.

REQUIREMENTS

• Android 8.0 (Oreo) or newer
• A Google or email/password account
• Internet for sync (offline queue catches everything in between)
